Getting Started

1. Create an Account

Sign up for a free account to access all scanning features, save history, and export reports.

2. Choose Your Scanning Method

We offer four ways to check for phishing:

  • URL Scanner — Paste a single URL for instant analysis
  • Email Scanner — Paste email content to extract and check all URLs
  • QR Scanner — Upload a QR code image to decode and verify the URL
  • Batch Check — Upload a CSV/TXT file with up to 100 URLs
3. Review Results

Get an instant verdict with confidence score, feature analysis chart, and threat intelligence from multiple sources.

Scanning URLs

How to scan a URL:
  1. Go to Scan Tools → Single URL in the navigation
  2. Paste the full URL (must start with http:// or https://)
  3. Click "Analyze URL"
  4. Review the results, including ML prediction and threat intelligence
Tip: You can also use the Quick Check on the homepage without logging in!

Email & QR Scanning

Email Scanner

Paste the full email content including headers, body, and any links. ShieldGuard Pro will automatically extract all URLs and check each one.

QR Scanner

Upload an image containing a QR code. We'll decode it, extract the URL, and check if it's safe. Supports PNG, JPG, and GIF formats.
